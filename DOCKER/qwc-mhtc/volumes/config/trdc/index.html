Search & online services

Use the main site to search for information, advice and guidance. Typical online tasks include reporting a missed bin, checking bin collection day, “Pay for it”, and viewing all online services — start from the homepage and menus above.

Contact us

We aim to respond within 10 working days. Payments, applications, requests and fault reporting are available through a single sign-on at my.threerivers.gov.uk.

You can contact us online, reach the right team for media, Council Tax, business rates, compliments and complaints, and find your local councillor — all via forms and pages on the main website.

Call centre: 01923 776611
Monday–Thursday 8:30am–5:30pm, Friday 8:30am–5pm (excluding Bank Holidays). Calls may be recorded and monitored.

Visitor centre: 9am–4pm Monday–Friday (excluding Bank Holidays) at Three Rivers House, Northway, Rickmansworth, Herts WD3 1RL.

Emergency contacts

In an emergency call 01923 776611. In working hours you reach Customer Services; outside those hours calls transfer to an out-of-hours service (Hertfordshire Constabulary) for emergency situations only.

News & social media

For council news and community information, follow Three Rivers District Council on social channels — links are in the footer of www.threerivers.gov.uk (e.g. Twitter / X and Facebook).

Languages & accessibility

If English is not your first language and you need help with a letter, document or bill, the council can assist. For website translations use the online contact options. The main site also offers Accessibility tools in the menu bar to translate pages.
